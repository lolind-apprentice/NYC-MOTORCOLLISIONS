    This tab is designed to explore the vehicles that frequently incur into crashes.

    Top 15 Vehicles Types Involved in NYC Collisions

    This bar chart displays triage 15 most frequently reported vehicle types involved in collisions. Passenger Vehicles account for the highest number of incidents, followed by Station Wagons/SUVs and Sedans, which suggests that the most common private vehicles on the road are also the most frequently involved in crashes. The presence of bicycles and motorcycles although numerically at a lower number highlights the vulnerability of smaller, less protected road users in high traffic settings.

    Top 15 States by Vehicle Registration in New York City Crashes

    This bar graph displays the distribution of vehicle registrations involved in crashes, categorized by where the vehicles were registered. With New York City registered vehicles accounting for the highest number of 800. This suggests that local residents are involved in more collisions than out of state drivers or tourists.

    Heatmap of Initial Impact vs. Major Vehicle Damage

    >

    This heatmap visualizes the relationship between the initial point of impact and the primary area of vehicle damage during accidents. In many cases, the location of the impact corresponds directly to where the major damage occurs. The data shows that the most common type of impact is to the center front of the vehicle, which also results in the highest frequency of damage to that same area. This suggests that front-end collisions are the most common and lead to damage in the front section.
